<h2>JavaScript setSeconds()</h2>

<p>The setSeconds() method sets the seconds of a date object (0-59):</p>

<p id="demo"></p>

<script>
var d = new Date();
d.setSeconds(30);
document.getElementById("demo").innerHTML = d;
</script>
