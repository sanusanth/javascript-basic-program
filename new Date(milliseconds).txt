<h2>JavaScript new Date()</h2>

<p>Using new Date(milliseconds), creates a new date object as January 1, 1970, 00:00:00 Universal Time (UTC) plus the milliseconds:</p>

<p id="demo"></p>

<script>
var d = new Date(0);
document.getElementById("demo").innerHTML = d;
</script>
